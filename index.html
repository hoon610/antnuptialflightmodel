Did you know?

All weather data is sourced from weatherapi.com. The likelihood of ongoing nuptial flights is calculated using a model that combines this weather data with historical nuptial flight data. Historical flight data for various ant species, including Camponotus, Formica, Pheidole, Temnothorax, Tetramorium, Lasius, Aphaenogaster, Myrmica, and Atta, is utilized. Flight data was obtained from the Asociación Ibérica de Mirmecología via AntFlights.com.
